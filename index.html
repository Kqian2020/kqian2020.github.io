Biogeraphy

In 2020, I received an M.S. degree from the School of Computer and Information, Anqing Normal University, Anqing, China, supervised by Professor Yusheng Cheng. Now, I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science, Southwest Petroleum University, Chengdu, China, under the supervision of Professor Fan Min.

My research interests include data mining, multi-label learning, and machine learning.
