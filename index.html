Biogeraphy

In 2020, I received the M.S. degree from the School of Computer and Information, Anqing Normal University, Anqing, China, supervised by Professor Yusheng Cheng. In 2024, I received the Ph.D. degree from the School of Sciences, Southwest Petroleum University, Chengdu, China, supervised by Professor Fan Min. Now, I am a Postdoctor in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Anhui University, Hefei, China, under the supervision of Professor Shu Zhao.

My research interests include data mining, multi-label learning, and machine learning.
